About EcoSpark

EcoSpark is a student-focused platform designed to promote eco-friendly actions across schools in the UAE. Our goal is to encourage environmental responsibility by awarding points for verified real-world activities.

For Students

Earn points for environmental actions, compete with classmates on leaderboards, and unlock achievement badges.

For Schools

Foster environmental awareness, track participation, and showcase your school's sustainability efforts.

For the UAE

Join the national movement towards sustainability and see how students are making a real difference.
